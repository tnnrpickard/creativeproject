Welcome to Modeling Club!

Welcome to the local modeling club! We are interested in all sorts of models:

  • Trains
  • Rockets
  • Figures
  • AND MORE!!
We meet in the basement of the community center at 7:00 pm on Thursdays, in the blue room. We host all different levels of modelers, from those just getting started to those who have been collecting for decades. Whether your interest is in medieval simulation, civil war dioramas, or miniature space shuttles, we hope to see you soon!!
